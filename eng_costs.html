Costs

Here you will find information on the costs of the camp, the camp t-shirt, accomodation and meals.

The Camp

Swing Spring 2013 includes:

  • four full days of classes from Thu to Sun: lindy hop and elective classes every day
  • four parties: Wed, Thu, Sat & Sun, including live music
  • two competitions: Jack’n’Jill on Thu and Strictly Lindy Hop on Sat
  • surprises, performances, discussions etc.

Price: 160€ + 10€, the membership fee of Swing Dance Society Break ry.
There is a 10 € discount if you register as a couple.

Discounts for the first to register:

  • the first 4 leaders and 4 followers 110€
  • 6 next ones 120€
  • 8 next ones 130€
  • 10 next ones 140€
  • 12 next ones 150€

Beginners

We will also be giving a lindy hop beginners class. The lessons will take place on Friday night before the party and on Saturday during day time. The price will also include all parties. Price: 60€ + 10€, the membership fee of Swing Dance Society Break ry. There is a 10€ discount if you register as a couple.

The Camp T-Shirt

Get yourself the fantastic Swing Spring 2013 camp t-shirt! Separate models for men and women are available in fresh spring green hue with the Swing Spring 2013 logo at the front of the shirt. The material is 100% high quality cotton. You can order yours when registering, the price is only 10€!

Accomodation

You need to book your accommodation straight from HimosLomat. At the time of reservation mention that you will be taking part in the Swing Spring dance camp.

We recommend that you gather or join a group of 5 to 7 people using eg. Facebook. All cottages have saunas and well-equipped kitchens. You can also stay at the Himos Hotel.

  • Säyrylä, for 6 people
    Price: 220€/the whole camp or 90€/one night
  • Koivula, for 5-6 people
    Price: 220€/the whole camp or 90€/one night
  • Himos Easy, for 7 people
    Price: 280€/the whole camp or 135€/one night
  • Himos Hotel, single room
    Price: 187€/the whole camp or 55€/one night
  • Himos Hotel, double room
    Price: 233€/the whole camp or 68€/one night

Cottage prices include pillows and blankets, but no bed linen. Hotel prices include breakfast at the hotel and guest sauna at evening time. Additional fees:

  • BED LINEN
    Bed linen set including a towel 12€, or 10€ for 4 people or more. The linen must be ordered by 12pm one day before your arrival.
  • CLEANING
    If you don’t want to spend time on cleaning the cottage, you can have Himos do it for you! Cleaning in 6 ppl cottages 62€, in 8 ppl cottages 82€.
  • CHECK OUT LATER
    If you would like to take a shower or use the cottage kitchen after classes on Sunday, it is possible to check out at 6pm instead of 12pm for 50€/cottage, 30€/hotel room.

Meals

You can enjoy delicious food at Restaurant Tupa at Himos Hotel. It is possible to take all your meals there:

  • Breakfast buffet 8€
  • Lunch buffet: salad buffet, main course, bread and non-alcoholic drinks 12€
  • Dinner buffet: salad buffet, two main course options, bread, drinks 18€

You can book your meals while registering and pay for them at the restaurant.

You can also cook at your cottage, each of which has a well-equipped kitchen: fridge, cookware & tableware, oven, stove, microwave and dishwasher.

In Jämsä city centre you can find supermarkets and restaurants. There is also a small market at Himos Center.
