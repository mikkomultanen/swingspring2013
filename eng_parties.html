Parties

There will be total of four nights of dancing during Swing Spring 2013, starting from Wednesday until Saturday! During this time we'll also have exciting competitions and performances. Our official camp band will be Jimbino Vegan and the Jazz Cannibals from New Orleans. Many dancers know Jimmy from his earlier visits to Finland with the band called Too Dumb To Die. In addition to them we will have the very best swing DJs in Finland playing for you!

  • Wed 8/5 Swing Spring 2013 Warm-up Party!
  • Thu 9/5 Jack’n’Jill Competition
    Jimbino Vegan and the Jazz Cannibals
  • Fri 10/5 Teacher's Jam
    Jimbino Vegan and the Jazz Cannibals
  • Sat 11/5 Strictly Lindy Hop Competition
    Jimbino Vegan and the Jazz Cannibals

Party pass

The party pass allows you to take part in taster classes and the great parties on all nights. It costs 60€ + 10€, the membership fee of Rock'n'Swing Dance Club Break ry.

Party pass includes:

  • three tasters: Thu, Fri & Sat
  • four parties: Wed, Thu, Fri & Sat, including live music
  • two competitions: Jack’n’Jill on Thu and Strictly Lindy Hop on Sat
  • surprises, performances, discussions etc.

You can also get single tickets to each party from the door. The price is 10€ on Wed and 20€ on Thu, Fri and Sat. On Thu and Sat, you can take part in the competitions. Taster classes are not included.
